Respond to a Cyber Attack

Immediate guidance for individuals who have experienced cyber crime or online fraud

Been targeted by cybercriminals?

Don't panic. Taking the right steps quickly can help minimize damage and begin your recovery process. Support and resources are available to help you.

Time is Critical

The first few hours after discovering a cyber attack are crucial. Acting quickly can prevent further damage and improve your chances of recovery. Follow these steps in order of priority.

Immediate Steps to Take

1

Secure Your Accounts

Protect your accounts from further unauthorized access immediately.

  • ✓ Change passwords on all affected accounts
  • ✓ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • ✓ Log out of all sessions on compromised accounts
  • ✓ Check account settings for unauthorized changes
  • ✓ Review recent login activity
2

Disconnect & Isolate

Prevent the attack from spreading to other devices and networks.

  • ✓ Disconnect affected devices from internet
  • ✓ Turn off Wi-Fi and unplug network cables
  • ✓ Isolate infected devices from shared networks
  • ✓ Don't use USB drives between devices
  • ✓ Keep devices powered on for investigation
3

Document Everything

Collect evidence for reporting and potential legal proceedings.

  • ✓ Take screenshots of suspicious messages
  • ✓ Record all transaction details and amounts
  • ✓ Note exact dates and times of incidents
  • ✓ Save email headers and URLs
  • ✓ Keep records of all communications
4

Contact Financial Institutions

Protect your finances and limit financial damage immediately.

  • ✓ Call bank fraud hotlines immediately
  • ✓ Report unauthorized transactions
  • ✓ Request account freezes if necessary
  • ✓ Cancel compromised cards
  • ✓ Set up fraud alerts
5

Report the Incident

Report to authorities to help investigate and prevent others being targeted.

  • ✓ File complaint on cybercrime.gov.in
  • ✓ Contact local cybercrime police
  • ✓ Report to relevant online platforms
  • ✓ Inform your employer if work accounts affected
  • ✓ Report to credit bureaus if identity theft
6

Clean & Secure Systems

Remove malware and strengthen your security defenses.

  • ✓ Run full antivirus scans
  • ✓ Update all software and operating systems
  • ✓ Install security patches immediately
  • ✓ Remove suspicious programs
  • ✓ Consider professional device cleaning

Remember During Recovery

  • Recovery is a process - be patient and persistent
  • Keep all documentation organized and accessible
  • Cyber criminals target everyone - don't feel embarrassed
  • Use this as an opportunity to strengthen your security practices
  • Seek professional help for complex financial or legal issues
  • Stay vigilant for follow-up scam attempts
