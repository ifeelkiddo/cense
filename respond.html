Disclaimer*

This search database is created on the basis of cybercrime complaints received from the public. Indian Cybercrime Coordination Centre (I4C) does not certify the authenticity of complaints which are a matter of investigation with the local police authorities. This database is not complete as cybercriminals are known to rapidly create, change and dispose such identifiers. This database grows with increase in reporting of cybercrime. I4C agrees that there are chances of error in the reports which have been used to create this database, so user discretion is advised. If a user disagrees with the mention of a particular identifier on this database, he/she may send an email at cyberdost[at]mha[dot]gov[dot]in. duly explaining and substantiating his claim.
